History

Founded with a passion for exploration and a love for the great outdoors, MariVis Adventures was born from a simple idea: to bring people closer to the beauty of nature and the thrill of adventure. What started as a personal journey of discovery quickly evolved into a full-fledged adventure tourism company, dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences for travelers seeking excitement, challenge, and breathtaking landscapes.

At MariVis Adventures, we believe that every journey should be more than just a trip—it should be an adventure that awakens the soul. From mountain trails and jungle expeditions to extreme sports and cultural explorations, we carefully craft each experience to ensure our adventurers feel the rush of discovery while staying safe and connected with nature.
