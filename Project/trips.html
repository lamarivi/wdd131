Our Featured Trips

Trip to Canaima

Explore the breathtaking landscapes of Canaima, home to lush jungles, stunning lagoons, and the world’s tallest waterfall, Angel Falls.

Trip to the Dessert

Walk through Venezuela’s only desert, where golden sand dunes meet the blue sky in a surreal landscape.

Trip to El Avila

Escape to the lush mountains of El Ávila, Caracas' natural paradise. Hike through cloud forests, take a cable car ride to the top, and enjoy panoramic views of the city and the Caribbean Sea.

Trip to Merida

Discover the beauty of the Andes with a visit to Mérida, where you can ride the world’s highest cable car, explore charming mountain villages, and enjoy breathtaking views and adventure sports
